    Frequently Ask Quetions

    How can Frias keep their prices so affordable?
    We buy large quantities of the same garments direct from the supplier, with few middlemen, and sell the garments in our own stores. Frias has considerable experience of the textile industry and a great knowledge of which goods to buy from which markets. In addition, we have invested a lot in a fast and cost-efficient distribution system. Frias is also permeated by cost-consciousness at every level, allowing us to offer goods to our customers at the very best prices possible
    Facebook and Instagram is offering me a gift card that supposedly is to be valid at Frias. Is this really true?
    Unfortunately, a number of fake campaigns and Frias accounts are circulating on Facebook and Instagram giving away gift cards to our customers. We are aware of the problem and Frias is not the only company afflicted. We do not support these kinds of actions and we always report them to Instagram and Facebook to get them closed down as soon as possible and prevent them from spreading even further. We truly apologize for the inconvenience that these fake campaigns may cause our customers. You will find information about any future contests via our official Instagram (@frias), Facebook (facebook.com/frias), Twitter (twitter.com/frias) and Google+ (plus.google.com/+frias).
    I responded to an email and/or online questionnaire to enter a contest. Is this an Frias initiative or is it fraudulent?
    Frias campaigns is promoted through our website. Frias cautions you to read the fine print and legal disclaimers when entering online contests. You will note in most situations that the promotion is in no way associated with our brand. If you have entered your mobile phone number in response to an email for a chance to win an Frias contest, are a current victim of such an action, or are receiving unwanted SMS text messages, please contact your phone provider and block the offending phone number.
