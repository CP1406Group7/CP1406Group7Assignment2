Mission Statement

The main goal of this website is to provide an online storefront of the store to improve the client's range of service, and to provide a medium for the customers to get themselves updated on the client's new products and services (for example the café and upcoming events), promotions, and other announcements. Moreover, the availability of the website would increase the number of people who are aware of the client's store, which would we possible by the presence of sharing buttons linked to social networks, where customers could share about the store.

Success Evaluation

The success of the website can be measured by the increase of customers after a given time (for example 1 to 2 months) after the website's release, and the number or online transactions.

Target Audience

The website will be designed in a child-friendly manner to attract children, yet easy to use for the adults who would be the customers.
